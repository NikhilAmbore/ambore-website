FAQ

Common questions

Ambore is a free, all-in-one AI career platform with three tools: a Job Portal with 1,000+ daily-updated tech jobs, an AI Resume Builder that tailors your resume to any job description, and an AI Interview Co-Pilot that gives you real-time answers during interviews.
Jobs are automatically scraped from 5 sources: USAJobs.gov (government tech jobs), Adzuna (aggregates Indeed, Monster, etc.), The Muse (top tech companies), Remotive (remote jobs), and Google Jobs. New listings are added every 24 hours.
Upload your existing resume and paste the job description. The AI analyzes both, then rewrites your resume to highlight relevant skills, match keywords, and optimize formatting for ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems). You get a tailored resume in seconds.
Ambore runs entirely in your browser. There are no extensions, no screen sharing, and no external indicators. It works with Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and in-person interviews ‚Äî as long as your mic picks up the question.
Everything is free ‚Äî no credit card, no trial limits. All personal data (resume, sessions, account) is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to external servers beyond the AI API calls needed to generate answers and resumes.
